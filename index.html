Our Story

“Manzil” means destination. For us, it’s the cozy corner where steam curls from clay cups and conversation lingers. We partner with small gardens across Assam, Darjeeling, Nilgiris, and the Western Ghats to bring you seasonally fresh leaves and beans.

  • Direct relationships with growers and cooperatives
  • Transparent supply chain and fair pay commitments
  • Blends developed in-house for authentic regional profiles
